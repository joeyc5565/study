ReVISit Bar Chart Experiment

About the Experiment

This experiment is designed to study how different bar chart orientations affect a person's ability to interpret data. Participants will be shown three different bar chart orientations and will be asked to determine the value of the fifth column in each case.

How It Works

  1. Participants will be presented with three types of bar charts (one per page), each in a different orientation (vertical, horizontal, and reverse).
  2. For each chart, participants must estimate and input the value of the fifth column.
  3. The values will always be an integer in between 0 and 100.
  4. Responses will be collected and analyzed to determine how orientation influences perception.
